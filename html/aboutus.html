Fact

In order to improve the economy of India, agricultural growth needs to levitate. This demands small and marginal scale agriculture to become efficient and self-sustaining. With an 85% contribution to Indian agriculture, small farmers can directly impact the future of sustainable agricultural growth and food security of the country. Which is why, there needs to be a paradigm shift in the way they function. But despite nearly 65% Indians working in the farm sector, India’s agricultural yields trail global and emerging market averages. More than 20 crore Indian farmers have little or no access to mechanized tools. Any effort to bring about a paradigm shift in productivity and farm income has to be centred on the small farmers, who account for 86% of India's farmland holding

What We Do

This platform brings the benefits of the technology-enabled sharing economy to boost the income of Indian farmers. This app facilitates the hiring of tractors and modern farm machinery to farmers, free of cost. Farmers/Dealers looking to rent their existing tractors and farm equipment are linked directly to farmers seeking to hire them through the “Farmer-to-Farmer model” (F2F) of Machinery Rental System app which enables them to contact farmer/dealer fulfill their respective requirement.
Farmers who own small parcels of land across India can hire state-of-the-art farm equipment to increase their productivity and income significantly. The App and on-ground Kiosk Centers which are operated by tractor and equipment owners will provide farmers with affordable farm mechanization services in a transparent manner.

What We Offer

We offer 2 things:-

  1. For the farmers, especially economically weak farmers, we provide machinery that they can not afford to buy on rent, which in turn will increase their productivity.
  2. For those who have spare machinery and want to rent out them, we provide them oppertunity to list theor machines , help the poor farmers along with earning some money.
